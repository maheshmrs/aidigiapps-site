Privacy Policy — AI Chess

Effective date: 12 July 2026

AI Chess (“the app”, “we”, “us”), published by AiDigiApps, is a chess app for iOS. It lets you play and analyze chess against the app's own built-in engines, against a cloud AI using your own API key, or against a chess model that runs entirely on your device. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. AI Chess is built to keep your data on your device and under your control.

In short: There is no account and no sign-in. When you choose a cloud AI, the chess position is sent directly to the provider you pick, using your key — we don't operate that model or receive your data on our servers. The on-device model works fully offline. We don't sell your data, run ads, or use third-party trackers.

Information the app handles

AI provider API keys (BYOK)

To play against a cloud AI, you supply your own API key for the provider you select (for example OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, Google, Meta, Microsoft, DeepSeek, or Moonshot). Keys are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device and are sent only to that provider to authenticate your requests. We never receive or store your keys on our servers.

Chess positions sent for AI moves

When you play against, or request a hint from, a cloud AI, the app sends the current board position, the recent move list, and the legal moves for that turn directly to the AI provider you chose so it can return a move. That processing is governed by that provider's own privacy policy. We are not a party to it and do not receive or retain that data. The app sends no personal information (such as your name) to providers.

On-device AI model

If you choose the on-device engine, the chess model runs entirely on your device — nothing about your game is sent anywhere while you use it. The model file itself is downloaded once, on your request, from a public model host (Hugging Face); that download is a standard file transfer and contains no personal data about you.

Built-in engines

The app's own from-scratch chess engines run fully offline on your device and send no data anywhere.

Local settings and saved games

Your preferences, optional player name, and any games you save (as PGN files) are stored locally on your device. When you Save or E-mail a game, iOS's own share sheet or Mail composer opens and you choose where it goes; the app does not transmit those files on its own.

Purchases

AI Chess is free to install, with a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks unlimited games. The purchase is processed by Apple's In-App Purchase. We do not receive or store your payment card or financial details. Whether the unlock is active is determined on your device from your Apple account entitlement; your free-game count is kept locally on your device.

Catalog requests to AiDigiApps

The app fetches a small model-pricing list and on-device model catalog from aidigiapps.com so it can show up-to-date prices and available models. These are simple downloads and send no personal data. Standard web-server logs (such as IP address) may be recorded by the host as part of serving the file; we do not use them to identify or track you.

Diagnostics

The app does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

How information is used

  • To send your move requests to the AI provider you selected and return the result.
  • To store your settings, saved games, and API key locally on your device.
  • To show current model pricing and available on-device models.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing.

Data sharing

  • AI providers you choose: receive the chess position/move data you submit, to generate a move. Review the privacy policy of the provider whose key you use.
  • Model host (Hugging Face): serves the on-device model file if you download it.
  • Apple: distributes the app.
  • We do not share your data with anyone else except as required by law.

Data retention and deletion

  • On-device data (your key, settings, saved games, and any downloaded model) is removed when you delete the app, or you can remove items in-app — remove a saved API key, or delete the downloaded on-device model — at any time.
  • We hold no personal data about you on our servers, so there is nothing for us to delete on our side.

Children

AI Chess is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Security

API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain. Network requests use HTTPS/TLS. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we use standard, industry-accepted protections.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the “Effective date” above will change accordingly. Material changes will be reflected here.
