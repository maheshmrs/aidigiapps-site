Frequently asked questions

What happens after my free trial ends?

Every new account gets a one-time 30-day trial of Guru. When it ends, the app drops back to core logging, streaks, and History — you can pick a Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly plan anytime from Settings → Sadhana Guru to continue.

Do I need an API key to use the app?

No — habit logging, streaks, and History work without one. An API key is only needed if you turn on AI Summary, which uses your own Anthropic or OpenAI key to generate a written summary of your practice log.

Where does my AI Summary data go?

Only when you tap Generate: the relevant portion of your practice log for that period is sent directly to the provider you chose (Anthropic or OpenAI), using the key you supplied. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Why can't I log a habit yet?

Habits are time-bound — a habit set for the evening won't accept an entry in the morning. Habits scheduled at 18:00 or later get a next-morning window, so you can still log last night's practice before noon.

I deleted a habit — did I lose its history?

No. Deleting a habit archives it rather than erasing it — its past logs stay exactly as they were in History and Reports, reconstructed for the days it was actually active.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed by Apple or Google, not by us. On iOS, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions; on Android, open the Play Store → Payments & subscriptions. You can also tap Restore purchases on the Guru screen if a purchase isn't showing.

How do I turn off sound or ambient audio?

Open Settings → Sound to turn off tap/completion sounds, or to turn off or change the ambient track (Calm, Om, a flute preset, or your own audio file URL).

How do I delete my account?

Email support@aidigiapps.com from the address on your account and we'll delete your account and associated practice data. This does not cancel an active subscription — cancel that separately in your Apple or Google subscription settings first.
