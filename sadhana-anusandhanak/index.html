What it does

Built around how a real practice actually happens — in sessions, at particular times, not one box a day.

🪔

Made for devotional habits

Chanting, Reading, Hearing, Prayer, Meditation, Service, Diet, Sleep and more — categories that speak your practice's language, not a generic wellness app's.

⏱️

Time-bound, honestly

Set a habit for the evening and it won't let you log it at breakfast. Late-night practices like sleep get a next-morning window so you can still record last night.

😊

Mood and notes, per session

Log more than a number — how you felt, and what book, meal, or music went with it. Chant four rounds across the day and each one is its own entry.

📅

History, on a real calendar

Go back and log or edit any past day. History reconstructs exactly which habits were active that day, even ones you've since retired.

✨

AI Summary — bring your own key

Ask for a weekly, monthly, or yearly summary written from your own practice log. Add your own Anthropic or OpenAI key in Settings — we never see it after you save it.

🎧

A calmer app to hold

A soft tap and a completion chime, an optional ambient drone or flute while you practice, and a translucent, native-feeling interface throughout.

Private by design

Your practice is personal. We treat it that way.

🔐

Your AI key stays yours

AI Summary only ever talks to the provider you chose, using the key you supplied. We store it to make requests on your behalf, but never show it back to you or anyone else.

🗂️

History, never erased

Deleting a habit archives it instead of destroying its past logs, so your history stays accurate even as your practice changes over time.

🚫

No trackers, no ad SDKs

No advertising SDKs, no third-party analytics, no selling your data. Read the privacy policy.
